Puppies are #1!

Puppies come from various backgrounds. They are seperated from their mother's no sooner than 6 weeks. We want to make sure they are well taken care of before they need to be seperated from the mother. This helps with anxiety and biting with their siblings. They learn to not bite as much when they play with their mother and siblings to prevent from biting when in training.
